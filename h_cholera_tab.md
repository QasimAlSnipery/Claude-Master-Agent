## Cholera — Revision Table

| Bacterium | Detail |
|---|---|
| Organism | **Vibrio cholerae** (comma, G-ve) |
| Pathogenic serogroups | **O1, O139** |
| Biotypes | Classical, **El-Tor** (7th pandemic) |

| Pathogenesis | Detail |
|---|---|
| Mechanism | **Enterotoxin → cAMP** → fluid secretion |
| Invasion | **Non-invasive** (no RBC/WBC in stool) |

| Transmission | Faecal-oral, water/food |
|---|---|
| At risk | Children, elderly, **blood group O**, low gastric acid |
| Incubation | Hours–5 days (typically 2–3) |

| Clinical | Detail |
|---|---|
| Hallmark | **Rice-water painless diarrhoea**, fishy odour |
| Fever | **Absent** (present in children) |
| Severe | ≥10% weight loss, shock |
| Children | Hypoglycaemia → convulsions |

| Diagnosis | Stool microscopy, **TCBS agar** |
|---|---|

| Treatment | Detail |
|---|---|
| Priority | **Rehydration** |
| Severe | **IV Ringer lactate** |
| Mild-mod | **ORS** (80% treatable) |
| Antibiotic (adult) | Doxycycline/tetracycline |
| Antibiotic (child/pregnant) | **Azithromycin** |
| Zinc | Children, 2 weeks |

| Mortality | Untreated severe **50%**; rehydrated <2% |
|---|---|

| Vaccine | Dukoral, Shanchol, Vaxchora (oral) |
|---|---|

**Bold = high-yield.**
