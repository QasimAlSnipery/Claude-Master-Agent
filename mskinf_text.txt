Septic Arthritis
Definition: is a condition of infection of a joint by
pus forming microorganism, the joint can become
infected by :
• Direct invasion through a penetrating wound ,intra
articular injection or arthroscopy .
• Direct spread from adjacent bone abscess
,especially in very young infant in whom the
growth plate is not an impenetrable barrier .

Osteomeylitis
Is infection of bone marrow by pus forming
microorganism, pyogenic microorganism
,which may be in acute or chronic form .
How do microorganisms reach the
Musculoskeletal tissues?
• Direct spread from a contiguous focus of
infection
• Indirect spread via the bloodstream

Definition and epidemiology
• Diabetic foot is a complication of long standing
diabetes mellitus in form of an infection/ulcer
below the level of the malleoli
• 30% of patients with diabetes mellitus has
peripheral neuropathy or angiopathy
• 25% of patients develop diabetic foot at some poin.
10/28/2025 Prof. Dr Omer A.R. Barawi 24
=====PAGE 25 [T]=====
Peripheral vascular disease
• Atherosclerosis affects mainly the medium-sized
vessels below the knee.
• Main signs and symptoms are pain and
claudication, skin is smooth and cold, pulses are
weak or absent
• Superficial ulcers occur at toes and deep ulcers at
pressure points heel and planter aspect of first
metatarsal (vascular ulcers are painful and tender)

Management
• The orthopaedic surgeon will usually be one
member of a multidisciplinary team
• Prevention: regular attendance at a diabetic
clinic, full compliance with medication,
examination for early signs of vascular or
neurological abnormality, advice on foot
care and footwear, and a high level of skin
hygiene.
